ABOUT US
HOW WE BEGIN

As the name suggests, HEIST INN is a cafe and restaurant based on the theme of the Netflix show Money Heist. Not just that, just like the show the core values we cherish the most are faith and friendship. We aspire to build a positive relationship with each one of our customers. Our team and staff are dedicated, talented, and have the best interests of the customers at heart. We always plan to serve our customers with the best quality of food and service every time. Our customers are the family that we connect with through HEIST INN and we treat them like one as well.

OUR GOAL

Our goal with heist inn has been to build an establishment from where no one goes disappointed. We don't just serve food to our customers, it comes with the best of ingredients, the hard work, and the talent of our staff. Step into HEIST INN for a mesmerizing experience that will make you want to visit us again and again
